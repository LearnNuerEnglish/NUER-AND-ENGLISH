Thok Naath Time Phrases

Essential time-related expressions in Nuer (Thok Naath) and English.

Nuer (Thok Naath) English Translation
Jɛn ɛ thaak di̱?What time is it?
Ɛ thaak di̱?What’s the time?
Ɛ thaak wäl.It’s ten o’clock.
Ɛ wäär där.It’s midnight.
Ɛ thaak bäkɛl ɛn tämɛ.It’s 6 A.M. now.
Ban jëk kɛ thaak bädäk.See you at 8 P.M.
Thaak dä cɛ cuɔ̱ŋ.My watch is stopped.
Thaak dä ɛ niy-niy.My watch is slow.
Ca thaak jiɛɛnä.It’s time to leave.
Te kɔn kɛ thaak mi̱ ro̱ŋ.We have plenty of time.
Gua̱a̱th cɛ we̱ ɛn tämɛ.It’s too late now.
