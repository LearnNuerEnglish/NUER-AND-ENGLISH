Thok Naath Time Phrases

A reference list for Nuer time-related expressions and clock hours.

Common Phrases

Nuer (Thok Naath) English Translation
Jɛn ɛ thaak di̱? What time is it?
Ban jëk kɛ thaak kɛl. See you at 1 o'clock.
Ɛ thaak bäkɛl ɛn tämɛ. It is 6 o'clock now.
Ɛ wäär där. It is midnight.
Thaak dä cɛ cuɔ̱ŋ. My watch has stopped.
Te kɔn kɛ thaak mi̱ ro̱ŋ. We have plenty of time.
Ca thaak jiɛɛnä. It is time to leave now.
